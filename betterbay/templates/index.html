Our Mission

The Better Boating Alliance is dedicated to identifying, aligning and assisting governmental, corporate, private and charitable stakeholders to enhance boating, boating safety, watersports, tourism, and coastal living by focusing on information and education, promoting cleanliness and sustainability, and supporting accessibility and usability in Rhode Island waters.

Modernization of Navigation and Safety Presentation

This presentation is available for clubs and boating groups state-wide for the purposes of continuing education, boater development and promoting safety across Narragansett Bay. There is no charge for it but the Better Boating Alliance does ask for a donation at the club/organizations direction.

Presented by Captain Kent Dresser, President of the Better Bay Alliance, Founder of Confident Captain, and a 28-year Safe/Sea towboat veteran. Kents unique experience and understanding of Narragansett Bay and her many users and types of boaters provides valuable insight as to how to stay safe in an ever-changing bay. This engaging and high-impact presentation is about 1-hour long and is designed to be presented as part of your club or organizations speaker series. Please contact us now for more information and to check availability. Help your members be as safe as possible while participating in club events and while out on their own. Contact the Better Bay Alliance today to make this valuable presentation at your club or organization.
